FAQ

We offer Carsome Certified cars which have been selected based on customer needs and wants. Our curated selection of cars offers you the best in value, quality, convenience and peace of mind.

Carsome Certified cars are quality used cars that have passed a stringent 175-point inspection to ensure it is free of major accident, frame, fire or flood damage. Carsome Certified cars also are reconditioned before being listed on the website and come with the Carsome Promise which includes a fixed price with no hidden fees, a 1-year warranty, and a 5-day money-back guarantee.

All cars listed on our website have undergone a thorough 175-point inspection which includes a short road test. The cars condition including all underlying issues made known to us will be transparently listed and will be restored during reconditioning of the car. Only cars that passed our inspection and have been checked for its vehicle history will be listed on our website.

Car listings labelled as "Carsome Certified" are owned by Carsome. However, cars labelled as Marketplace" are owned by our partners and have also undergone the 175-point inspection.

                Frequently Asked Question

                What other/special types of car do you buy?

                Cars owned by non-Malaysian citizens

                We do buy cars owned by non-Malaysian citizens. However, the process differs from Carsome’s standard procedures.

                 

                Non-Malaysian car owners have to go through the following processes:
                 

                1. Clear any outstanding bank loans and provide a Puspakom B5 report (transfer of ownership inspection process at Puspakom).
                2. Send the car to Carsome for inspection and finalise transaction (accept Carsome’s on-the-spot offer or opt for online bidding).  
                3. Perform the ownership transfer in person with Carsome’s inspector at JPJ’s (the Road and Transport Department of Malaysia) center, where the car owner’s thumbprint will be recorded.
                4. Wait for Carsome to issue payment.
                   

                Cars registered outside Malaysia

                At the moment, we only buy cars registered in Malaysia (including Sabah and Sarawak), except Labuan and Langkawi.

                 

                Cars registered under Police or Army ID

                The process for selling cars registered under Police or Army ID follows our standard procedures. Car owners with vehicles registered under Police or Army ID are required to update their IDs to the standard 12-digit NRIC number before proceeding.
                 

                Cars with engines over 3,000cc

                At the moment, we only accept the makes and models below:

                • Ford: Ranger, Trader
                • Honda: Accord, Elysion, LaGreat
                • Isuzu: Citation, D-Max
                • Mazda: BT-50, CX-9, MPV
                • Mitsubishi: Pajero, Storm, Triton
                • Nissan: Cefiro, Elgrand, Infiniti, Murano, Skyline, Urvan
                • Toyota: Alphard, Estima, Harrier, Hiace, Hilux, Land Cruiser
                   

                If you are uncertain about your make and model, please write to us at info@carsome.my or call us at 1800-82-3388 for more information.
                 

                Others

                We purchase vans, performance cars, and all reconditioned cars with company or individual approved permits (AP). If you are unable to find the car model on Carsome’s online appointment booking form, please write to us at info@carsome.my or call us at 1800-82-3388 to arrange for an inspection appointment.

                What happens after I agree to sell my car, and what do I need to prepare for ownership transfer?

                You need to release your car to us within 5 days from the day you accept our offer or the bid price. Carsome will contact you to arrange for the following:
                 

                • Provide thumbprint
                • Provide purchase invoice (for cars purchased by cash)

                 

                All paperwork for ownership transfer will be handled by Carsome on the agreed date of the vehicle handover. The remaining loan settlement with the bank and final payment will be made directly to the car owner’s bank account via online transfer.
                 

                We require the following documents and items for ownership transfer:

                • NRIC (or passport for non-Malaysians)
                • Original vehicle Registration Card (RC) / Original Vehicle Ownership Certificate (VOC)
                • Spare keys (if any)
                • Outstanding hire purchase loan statement (if any)
                   

                If we do not receive  your vehicle RC/VOC, we will withhold RM2,000 from the selling price as collateral. You must produce the RC/VOC within 14 days from the ownership transfer date or the RM2,000 will be forfeited.
                 

                What is the process of the inspection appointment?

                Notify your inspector-in-charge when you arrive for your inspection. The whole appointment should not take more than 30 minutes. Our professional, certified inspectors will conduct a thorough 175-point inspection of your car, which includes a short road test. The road drive helps us to determine the realistic value of your car. Photos will also be taken for record and reporting purposes.

                 

                We will offer you a price to buy your car on the spot. If you agree to accept the offer and sell to us, we will complete all the necessary paperwork for you, and pay you via online transfer within 1 hour*. If you decide to try online bidding, we will schedule your car for a 2-hour online bidding among our verified used car dealer network.  The starting bid price will be discussed and agreed upon between you and Carsome prior to the live bidding session.

                 

                *Subject to terms and conditions and receipt of all documents.
